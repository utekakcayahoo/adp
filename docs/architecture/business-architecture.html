Facility Energy & Carbon Co-pilot — Business Architecture

Who it serves, the decisions it supports, and the value it returns — on a foundation of facility data.

🎯 North star: hit the organization's emissions-reduction target (e.g. −50% by 2030) — by making every facility's carbon visible, explainable, and actionable.
1 · Who we serve
Primary

Sustainability Manager

Owns the portfolio target. Asks: "Are we on track? Which sites are dragging us down?"

Operator

Facility Manager

Runs one site. Asks: "Why did my building spike? What should I fix first?"

Audience

Executive / ESG Reporting

Needs a credible summary with an audit trail. Asks: "Give me the quarterly report."

▼
2 · What the co-pilot does (business capabilities)

📊 Measure & Baseline

Turn kWh of electricity & gas into tCO₂e (Scope 1 + Scope 2) per facility, per period.

🎯 Track vs Target

Compare current trajectory against reduction targets — org-wide and per site.

🔍 Diagnose

Explain spikes & anomalies using weather and usage patterns.

💡 Recommend

Prioritized actions to cut emissions and cost, biggest lever first.

📑 Report

Audit-ready, GHG/ESG-aligned narrative — generated on request.
